Country Children's House

Discover

Country Children's House is a Montessori school with two locations in Noblesville, Indiana.

We offer Preschool and Kindergarten classes at the Allisonville Road location and Preschool at our West location that follow the Montessori philosophy of teaching young children.

Mission Statement

With caring & compassion, we will provide a warm, caring, academic environment for the child, which prepares them for their next learning environment.

Vision

To be an academic based school, which uses Montessori concepts, philosophies and materials to meet the needs of the children in a kind and flexible manner.

Values

  • Respect: We value the contributions of each individual and encourage development of individual ideas.
  • Ethical: We value ethical behavior of all relationships - ourselves, parents, children and the other teachers.
  • Accountable: We value continued improvement in our program and our individual teaching. Therefore, we hold each other accountable.
  • Teamwork: We value our teaching environment that requires teamwork and honest communication.
  • Positive Attitude: We encourage a positive attitude between teachers, parents and children.
  • Quality: We strive to provide the best quality of education.
  • Compassionate: We value each child as an individual of great worth and dignity and seek to meet their needs with a nurturing, compassionate nature.
